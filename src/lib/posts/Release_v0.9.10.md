## Changelog

This release adds a small but long-requested bit of control over browsing: you can now choose how many results load on a page.

- A new **Per page** selector sits alongside the Sort controls, letting you switch between 10, 20, or 30 results at a time. Prefer a quick skim with fewer cards, or load more at once to scroll less — your call.
- The selector is available everywhere the asset grid appears: Browse, the community Playlists page, your own playlists, Favorites, the 343 Recommended page, and individual playlist pages.
- Your choice is reflected in the page's URL, so a link you share or bookmark opens with the same page size you picked.
- The control was designed to stay compact and out of the way, and it behaves properly on mobile so it never crowds the filter bar on smaller screens.
